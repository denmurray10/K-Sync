01

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide, such as your email address, display name, saved favourites, and optional profile preferences.
  • Usage signals including pages viewed, stream interactions, sign-up activity, and general session behaviour.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device data, IP-derived location region, and security logs used to protect the platform.

02

How We Use Information

  • Operate the station, maintain accounts, personalise key features, and improve the listener experience across the site.
  • Measure audience behaviour so we can understand which programming, pages, and tools are genuinely useful.
  • Detect abuse, prevent fraud, troubleshoot performance issues, and keep the service safe and stable.

03

Legal Basis And Consent

Where required, K-Beats relies on consent before enabling analytics or advertising-related storage. Essential processing for security, site delivery, and account operation may still run where it is necessary to provide the service.

You can review or change your consent settings at any time through the controls described in our Cookie Policy.

04

Sharing And Processors

We may work with trusted providers for hosting, analytics, infrastructure, media delivery, and operational support. They process data on our behalf under appropriate terms and only for the purposes described in this policy.

05

Your Controls
