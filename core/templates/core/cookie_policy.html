01

Cookie Categories

  • Strictly necessary: required for security, core site features, and keeping the service operational.
  • Analytics: helps us understand traffic, listener journeys, and which features earn repeat use.
  • Advertising: supports ad storage and personalisation where you have agreed to those preferences.

02

Consent Choices

The K-Beats consent banner lets you accept all, reject all, or save a more specific set of preferences. Your decision is stored so we can respect it on future visits until you change it again.

03

Google Consent Mode

K-Beats sends consent signals to Google tags so analytics and advertising storage stay denied by default until you grant permission. This helps our measurement stack respond to the choices you make instead of assuming consent.

04

Managing Cookies

You can reopen your preferences from the site controls at any time. Browser settings can also be used to block or remove cookies, although some parts of the experience may stop working properly if strictly necessary cookies are disabled.

05

More Information

For a broader explanation of how listener and account data is handled across K-Beats, read the Privacy Policy. For rules covering use of the service itself, see the Terms of Service.
