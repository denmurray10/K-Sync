About "SEVEN"

"SEVEN" by Jung Kook featuring Latto is a summer anthem that dominated global charts upon release. The track blends infectious pop hooks with hip-hop verses, showcasing Jung Kook's versatility as a solo artist. It spent multiple weeks at #1 on the Billboard Hot 100, making it one of the most successful K-Pop solo singles in history. The song's minimalist production and catchy chorus exemplify the evolving sound of fourth-generation K-Pop.
