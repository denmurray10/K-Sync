Editorial Overview

A Cleaner, More Personal Read

This redesign treats Jennie as the subject, not BLACKPINK as the default lens. The opening module centres her voice, solo identity, stage persona, and defining profile facts before moving into group context and discovery routes.

The structure is closer to a premium editorial profile: compressed portrait rail, headline-led hierarchy, grouped facts, selective accent color, and panels that scan like broadcast briefing notes instead of heavy wiki boxes.

A right-hand utility rhythm can hold quick facts, social links, and countdown or birthday status without overwhelming the main story. This keeps the interface professional, modern, and aligned with the homepage's live-channel atmosphere.
