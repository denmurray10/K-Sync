Our History

Founded in a small studio in Hongdae, K-Pop Pulse began as a passion project by three DJs obsessed with the Hallyu wave. Today, we broadcast from a state-of-the-art studio in Gangnam, partnering with major entertainment labels to bring exclusive interviews and comeback showcases.
