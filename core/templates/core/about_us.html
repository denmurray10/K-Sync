OUR
MISSION

What Drives Us

To transcend borders and language barriers through the universal language of music.

K-Beats is dedicated to bringing the authentic K-Pop experience to fans worldwide — curating not just the hits, but the stories behind the idols, the culture behind the craft, and the passion behind every note.

We syndicate high-velocity data from official Korean charts (Circle, Melon, Hanteo), music show results, and global streaming platforms to give fans a real-time pulse on the industry.

OUR
STORY

From a passion project to a global broadcast station.

2026

The Hongdae Spark

Three DJs obsessed with the Hallyu wave begin broadcasting from a tiny studio in Cornwall, UK. What starts as a weekend experiment quickly attracts a fiercely loyal online following.

arrow_forward
2020

Global Expansion

K-Beats hits 1 million listeners across 50 countries and launches its first official mobile app. International fan clubs begin forming in Europe, the Americas, and Southeast Asia.

arrow_forward
2023

Gangnam HQ Opens

We move into a state-of-the-art broadcast studio in Gangnam, partnering with major entertainment labels (HYBE, SM, JYP, YG) for exclusive comeback showcases and idol interviews.

arrow_forward
2025

K-Beats Is Born

A full platform relaunch introduces the K-Beats brand. Real-time AI-powered charts, live polls, interactive fan experiences, and the new editorial design language. 5 million listeners and growing.
