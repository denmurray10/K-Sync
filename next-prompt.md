Create a desktop-first web landing page hero for K-BEATS with an **explosive K-pop** aesthetic and immediate wow factor. This is the first thing users see, so it must feel like a comeback trailer frame.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, desktop-first (must still scale cleanly on mobile)
- Theme: Dark cinematic neon
- Background base: Deep black (#000000)
- Accent 1: Electric Cyan (#00f0ff)
- Accent 2: Neon Magenta (#f425c0)
- Text primary: White (#ffffff)
- Text secondary: Cool light gray (#cbd5e1)
- Visual language: Bold editorial typography, layered glows, dynamic color overlays, premium motion feel
- Surfaces: Transparent or glass-like only; no heavy opaque cards unless for contrast balance
- Borders: Thin, high-contrast, crisp
- Shadows/glows: Neon atmospheric glow, not muddy blur

**Critical constraints:**
1. Keep a full-width autoplay looping video as the **base background layer**.
2. Color overlays must sit **above** the video but still allow video detail to remain visible.
3. No player UI, no radio controls, no play buttons.
4. Include a transparent **Featured Article** module in hero (label + article title + read editorial affordance).
5. Keep the composition high-impact, energetic, and clearly K-pop.
6. No minimal aesthetic. This should feel explosive and expressive.
7. Typography must be oversized, stacked, and dramatic.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header/Nav:** Reuse an existing top nav style with transparent/dark treatment.
2. **Hero Background:** Full-viewport video with layered gradient/color effects and subtle texture.
3. **Hero Main Content:**
   - Kicker line (small uppercase tracking)
   - Massive 2–3 line headline with mixed filled + outlined styling
   - Short subtext line with confidence and hype tone
4. **Energy Details:**
   - Add subtle motion accents (light sweep / animated bars / directional glow)
   - Avoid clutter; details should reinforce rhythm and excitement
5. **Featured Article Module (Transparent):**
   - Label: “Featured”
   - Dynamic title placeholder text area
   - “Read Editorial” meta line with arrow icon affordance
6. **Bottom atmosphere strip:** A subtle lower gradient/lighting finish to anchor the hero.

**Interaction expectations:**
- Smooth hover transitions on featured module.
- Strong visual hierarchy from first paint.
- Hero remains legible over motion background.

**Output quality bar:**
- Must look premium enough to resemble a Dribbble/Awwwards-style concept.
- Must feel emotionally intense and polished, not generic.

---
💡 Optional implementation note: If generating HTML/CSS directly, ensure z-index layering is explicit (video at lowest layer, overlays above it, content highest), and keep featured module transparent with backdrop blur.
